FAQ

Questions we hear all the time

Do you come to my home or office? ▾
Yes! We offer both in-home and in-office visits across the Miami area, as well as remote support for most software issues. We'll let you know which option works best when you book.
What does "no fix, no fee" actually mean? ▾
If we can't resolve your issue, you don't pay for the service call. We'll be honest with you about whether we can fix something before we start — so you're never left with a bill and the same problem.
How fast can you get to me? ▾
We often have same-day availability, especially for remote support. For in-home visits, we can usually schedule within 24 hours. Urgent situation? Call us directly and we'll do our best.
Do you work on Macs too, or just Windows? ▾
Both! Most of our work is Windows-based, but we're fully comfortable with macOS troubleshooting, optimization, and hardware upgrades as well.
Will my data be safe? ▾
Absolutely. We never access your files beyond what's needed to fix the issue, and we always recommend a backup before any major work. Your privacy matters to us.
Can you help my small business with multiple computers? ▾
That's exactly what our Small Biz Plan is built for. We support teams of up to 20, including workstation setup, network config, shared drives, and ongoing maintenance. Reach out for a custom quote.
